How we started

Hira Naushad started making cards as a hobby. Being a student of software engineering at NED University of Engineering & Technology, the idea of making cards as a business seemed pretty farfetched. But since her handmade cards were very popular, she, along with her partners Hina Akram and Rijaa Shamsuddin, took the opportunity and put forward this idea. This was the beginning of Banwalo.com

Our aim
